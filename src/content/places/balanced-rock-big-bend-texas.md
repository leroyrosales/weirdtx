**Balanced Rock** is one of those **Big Bend National Park** stops that makes **Big Bend** feel bigger than the postcard — a little history, a little weird, a good excuse to pull over.

Details drift: hours, admission, and what’s still standing can change. Verify before you go, especially for rural pins and one-off attractions.
