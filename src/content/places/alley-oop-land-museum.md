**Alley Oop Fantasy Land** (often called **Alley Oop Land**) is a park-and-museum combo on the edge of **Iraan**, hometown of cartoonist **V.T. Hamlin**, who dreamed up the time-traveling caveman comic **_Alley Oop_** after digging into local dinosaur-era finds. The outdoor area is famous for **oversized figures**, Oop, Dinny, Oola, and friends, while the **Iraan museum** side folds in **Permian Basin oil history**, fossils, and regional artifacts.

The park is often **walkable from outside the gate** even when hours are odd; the **museum** usually keeps shorter seasonal hours (often afternoons, and not every day). **Call ahead** if you’re detouring from **I-10**, small-town attractions love to shift schedules without updating the whole internet.
