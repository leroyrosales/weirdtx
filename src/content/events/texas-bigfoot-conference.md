Run by the **Texas Bigfoot Research Conservancy** (Texas Bigfoot Research Center), this long-running conference draws speakers, field researchers, and the curious to the **Jefferson Tourism & Visitor Center** on East Austin Street. Expect talks, meet-and-greets, and a crowd that takes piney woods lore seriously.

Dates and ticket links move year to year — **confirm the current schedule on texasbigfoot.org** (or the organizer’s ticket page) before you book lodging in Jefferson.
